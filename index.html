~ How To Play~

Each player turns up a card at the same time and the player with the higher number card means they have a higher vibration and takes both cards to earn two points. If the cards are the same rank, you have entered a WAR and both players lose 4 points. The object of the game is to maintain your peace by having a higher vibration than your opponent. War indicates you a vibrating at a low frequency and fighting with another.

Can you keep the peace?
